RETREATS

In our ecolodge sanctuary, live this new way of enjoying your free time and renewing your energy with our health and nutrition workshops, therapeutic activities and eating organic consciously prepared food.
We are a peaceful retreat location which promotes well-being.
Our spaces are built based on the principle of bio-construction and geobiology, that put together art and science to design healthy sustainable homes. In this privileged relaxing and exclusive spot, you have everything you need to get acquainted with and enjoy Mother Nature.


Y O G A

The art of well-being to achieve balance and tranquillity.
All enhanced by the sacred architecture of our Dome.


P E R M A C U L T U R E

We have chosen to follow this humanistic environmental philosophy to preserve the organic garden and its flora and fauna, to supply energy to the ecosystem, to protect and respect both the landscape design and its social structures.


O R G A N I Z E   Y O U R   R E T R E A T

Tell us about your practice, either meditation, yoga, nutrition or wellness in general and make it the heart of your own retreat at Origines Lodge.


P E R S O N A L   D E V E L O P M E N T

We integrate several holistic approaches to offer you a place where you can reach the state of full consciousness and live a conscious life. A 360’ experience of awareness and meditation.


N A T U R O P A T H Y

Experience the unique healing approach of this alternative medicine which will bring a breath of fresh air into your life. Choose between a wide range of holistic massages to connect with and take care of your Self.


W O R K S H O P S
