LODGE

Fresh air, ocean breeze and a natural oasis just in front of you. All spaces are designed to blend in with the natural surroundings: the ceilings are injected with color, the presence of natural aromas and healing plants enhances the sense of peace and serenity. Carefully chosen interiors, textures, shapes, elegance and delicate natural lighting help you to have a positive emotional experience.

A C C O M M O D A T I O N

Wake up every morning to a sensorial experience thanks to the toasted aroma of 100% handmade ground coffee. Walk in spaces created with natural materials and inspired by the art of geobiology. Immersed in harmony rich in culture, civilization and biology. Enjoy creative delicacies grown in our organic gardens which are a conscious experience in an environment compatible with nature.
