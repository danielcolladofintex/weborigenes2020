LOCATION

Origines Lodge is located in a hidden, quiet spot near the tropical jungle of Playa Hermosa on the Pacific Ocean with a lovely tropical climate. We are a holistic center specialized in wellness retreats; a beautiful, healthy and special adventure to be experienced. San Jose international airport is only 90 minutes away by car.

COSTA   RICA



Meditation, yoga retreats, energy renewal, surfing, conscious organic food and exclusive hikes into the wild essence of Costa Rica.

PURA VIDA - PURE LIFE

We are just 30 minutes north of Manuel Antonio National Park. Famous for its incredible biodiversity, Manuel Antonio is the most visited National Park in Costa Rica. Here you will have the chance to observe unique mammals such as monkeys, sloths, and more than 200 species of colorful birds. Or you can visit untouched beaches, stunning waterfalls, try horse-therapy and many other healing experiences.

¿ FAQ ?

Where is Origenes Lodge located?

Playa Hermosa is a quiet surf community, located between Jacó and Manuel Antonio, about 90 minutes south of the San José International Airport (SJO). This location allows our guests to enjoy a wide range of activities and trips whenever they decide to venture outside the lodge area.

Is there any parking on the premises?

Yes, each house has two private parking spaces

What is the maximum number of guests that can be accomodated in each house?

The maximum capacity per house is 6 people.

Does the apartment have an equipped kitchen?

Yes it does, the kitchen is fully equipped.

Are pets allowed?

Unfortunately pets are not allowed in our facilities in order to preserve the local biodiversity and also for the safety of your pet. Remember, we are in the jungle!

Is there a swimming pool?

Yes there is. It is a beach-style pool that is easy to access. A part of the pool is also designed to be safer for children and those who you cannot swim.

Is there an airport nearby?

Yes there is. The closest airport is Juan Santamaría International Airport (SJO) and it is 90 minutes drive from Orígenes Lodge.

Do you provide a transfer service from the airport to the Lodge?

We are glad to offer transfer service to and from the airport. Please contact us in advance for a quote and availabilities.

Is breakfast included?

Yes it is included.

Are there pool towels available to guests?

We do have pool towels available to our guests.
